The record of the genealogy of Jesus the Messiah, the son (descendant) of David, the son (descendant) of Abraham: 
² Abraham was the father of Isaac, Isaac the father of Jacob, and Jacob the father of Judah and his brothers [who became the twelve tribes of Israel]. ³ Judah was the father of Perez and Zerah by Tamar, Perez was the father of Hezron, and Hezron the father of Ram.
